📥 Import M-Pesa PDF Statement / CSV / Excel

Hybrid engine: embedded-text parsing + Tesseract OCR fallback for scanned/image PDFs
📄 PDF statements: pages with a text layer parse instantly; image-only pages are automatically OCR'd with Tesseract.js (rendered at 2.5× for accuracy — first OCR run downloads the ~15 MB language model once, then it's cached). Handles Merchant Payment from / Online from / Small Business Pay Merchant from / received from rows.
🖼️ Images: PNG/JPG screenshots/photos are OCR'd. Contact lists are resolved first — if the image looks like a name+phone list, masked contacts are auto-unlocked. Otherwise, M-Pesa transactions are parsed. wrapped receipt numbers & multi-line names. OCR misreads of masked numbers (*** read as #**/xxx) are auto-repaired.
🛡️ Duplicate guard: every transaction fingerprinted by Receipt No (or date+time+phone+amount). Re-importing the same or overlapping statements skips already-loaded rows — flagged in preview.
📊 CSV / Excel: customer exports, transaction lists, or contact lists (auto-routed to the Resolver).
